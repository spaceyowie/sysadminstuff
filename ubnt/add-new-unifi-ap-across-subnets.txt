[[ A a new UniFi AP Pro to UniFi Controller on different subnet ]]

[1] Wire the UniFi AP Pro to the same subnet as the UniFi Controller - ensure there is a working DHCP server to give the correct IP.
[2] Discover the UniFi AP Pro using the Chrome add-on, the iOS Discovery app, or SSH to the UniFi AP Pro and issue the <...> command.
[3] Add the UniFi AP Pro to the UniFi Controller.
[4] Wire the UniFi AP Pro to the desired subnet.
[5] Check that the UniFi Controller can still control the UniFi AP Pro.
[6] Done.
